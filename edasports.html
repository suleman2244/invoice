    EDA Alerts

    In view of the recent increases in Covid-19 cases in several countries, including countries that were already in control of the disease, unfortunately, we do not consider it safe to maintain our tournament for this year. In addition, some teams mentioned some difficulty in reaching Egypt due to the temporary suspension of some flights to the region. For all these reasons, and in the hope that there will be a vaccine by the end of this year, as experts predict, sadly, we have decided to postpone the El Gouna tournament to a date to be announced soon.
